About

I am a Spider Mite. I have been called many things. Most of them uncouth, although very few of them were unearned. I can thrive in hot, dry conditions. I can lay 20 eggs every day and I only live for a month. I can cover plants with silky thread and with that same thread catch the wind and fly. I am a nuisance and a pest to those that find me. You may have heard of me.

Larva

Protonymph

  • Size: larger and more oval shaped than the larva
  • Color: deeper green
  • Shape: oval with four pairs of legs
  • Two dark spots are more pronounced

Deutonymph

  • Size: slightly larger than the protonymph
  • Shape: male can be distinguished from the female by its smaller size and more pointed abdomen

Service

OK now let's get into how to treat for these guys. They are really tricky to treat for, it turns out. They can reproduce really quickly. As a result of that they have a strong resistance to a lot of pesticides.

Location

Typically found on the underside of leaves. The webs will be everywhere, which we need to clean up with our brushes. But the mites themselves will be hiding underneath the leaves. Spraying wit the B&G on the underside of the leaves will be the best method. Be careful to not get any product on flowers! We still want to avoid hurting pollinators.

Inspection

I don't mean that kind of inspection. I mean looking at the plants to determine if they are actually effected by spider mites. Visually it can be hard to spot them since they are so small. One method is to take a piece of paper and hold it under the leaves and shake them. If the little black dots that fall on it start to move, then those are mites.

Pesticide

A pyrethroid like Suspend Polyzone will kill them. Since they primarily move from place to place by crawling on the ground, spraying underneath effected plants will help control them.

Insecticidal Soap

One option that we can tell customers is insecticidal soap. There are several different homemade recipes online to make this. All you need is a spray bottle, water and pure castile liquid soap. This soap is cheap and can be found on Amazon and at Target. If they don't want to make it, there are several brands like Safer that offer pre-made options.
